Fentynal

is a synthetic opioid that is up to 50 times stronger than heroin
and 100 times stronger than morphine. It is a major contributor to fatal and nonfatal overdoses worldwide.

80%

of drug overdoses are caused by fentanyl.
Drugs may contain deadly levels of fentanyl, and you wouldn’t be able to see it, taste it, or smell it. It is nearly impossible to tell if drugs have been laced with fentanyl unless you test your drugs with fentanyl test strips.

SIGNS OF OVERDOSE:

1: Small, constricted “pinpoint pupils.
2: Falling asleep or losing consciousness.”
3: Slow, weak, or no breathing.
4: Choking or gurgling sounds.
5:Limp body.
6:Cold and/or clammy skin.
7:Discolored skin (especially in lips and nails)
